About LabNeura

LabNeura is dedicated to developing efficient algorithms in C++ and exposing them through Python. We focus on implementing basic to advanced algorithms with a special emphasis on machine learning, reinforcement learning, and deep learning.

Mission

Our mission is to create a highly efficient and accessible library of algorithms that can be used by researchers, developers, and enthusiasts in various fields.

Vision

We envision a world where high-performance computing and machine learning algorithms are easily accessible to everyone, fostering innovation and technological advancement.
